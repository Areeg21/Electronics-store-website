Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our store and its features.

1. How do I sign up or log in?

Click on the “Sign Up” or “Login” button at the top of the homepage and fill in your details.

2. How do I view the products available?

On the homepage, you can browse all electronics or filter by category such as phones, laptops, or headphones.

3. How do I add items to the cart?

Click on the shopping cart icon next to any product you want to purchase to add it to your cart.

4. Can I manage products as an admin?

Yes, the admin dashboard allows you to view all products stored in the database.

5. Is there order tracking or payment?

Currently, the system does not support real-time order tracking or online payments. Orders are managed manually.
